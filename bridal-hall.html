Main Bridal Hall Decoration

Below are the decoration requirements for the bridal hall, followed by example images for inspiration.

Decoration Requirements:

  • Elegant floral arrangements on each side walls with cracks/damages and along the aisle.
  • Soft, warm lighting to create a welcoming atmosphere.
  • Pink gate with square pattern design.
  • A central stage with a backdrop matching the wedding theme colors.
  • Comfortable seating arrangements with decorative covers.
  • Selfie stand with silver-black cottons.
  • Flex to welcome near the konar hall
  • Carpets starting from konark hall to bridal hall
